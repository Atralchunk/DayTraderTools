About Day Trader Tools

Why this site exists and what we're trying to do.

What this site is

Day Trader Tools is a free resource built for active traders — particularly those working through prop firm evaluations or managing funded accounts. The tools, calculators and content here are designed to help traders think more clearly about risk, position sizing and consistency.

Everything on this site is free to use. No sign-up, no paywall, no ads.

Why it was built

After spending years trading — making the usual mistakes, blowing accounts, starting again — the one thing that made the biggest difference wasn't a better strategy or a smarter indicator. It was understanding risk properly.

The calculators on this site are the tools I wished existed when I was starting out. They're built around real questions traders actually face: what percentage should I risk given my current drawdown? What's the probability of passing this evaluation at this position size? How many days realistically will it take to hit my payout target?

The blog and education sections exist to share honest, practical experience — not hype, not impossible promises.
