Privacy policy

How Day Trader Tools handles your data.

Overview

Day Trader Tools is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it and your rights in relation to it.

Information we collect

Day Trader Tools does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the site. All calculators and tools run entirely in your browser — no data you enter into any calculator is sent to or stored on our servers.

We may collect basic anonymous usage data through analytics tools (such as page views and traffic sources) to help us understand how the site is being used and improve it over time. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies to improve your experience on the site — for example, to remember that you have acknowledged our disclaimer so it does not appear on every visit. We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites.

Third party services

This site uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. Google may collect limited data as a result of serving these fonts. We do not share any personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@daytradertools.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
